About Vignan's Foundation

**Vignan's Foundation for Science, Technology & Research** is a **deemed-to-be university** in **Guntur, Andhra Pradesh**. Established with a vision to create a center of excellence in **technical and scientific education**, the institution is known for its high standards of academic excellence and research-oriented programs.

The university provides a **diverse range of courses** in engineering, management, and applied sciences, ensuring students gain practical and theoretical knowledge. With state-of-the-art **labs, research centers, and industry collaborations**, Vignan's Foundation plays a key role in shaping the future of students.
